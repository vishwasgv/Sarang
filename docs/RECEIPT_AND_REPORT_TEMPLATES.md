## PAGE DIMENSION SPECIFICATIONS

### A4 Invoice / Report
- **Page size:** 210mm × 297mm
- **Margins:** Top 15mm, Bottom 15mm, Left 20mm, Right 20mm
- **Content area:** 170mm × 267mm
- **Font size (body):** 10–11pt
- **Font size (headings):** 13–16pt
- **Font size (footer):** 8pt
- **Line height:** 1.4
- **Business logo:** Max 40mm wide × 25mm tall

### A5 Report (Compact)
- **Page size:** 148mm × 210mm
- **Margins:** Top 10mm, Bottom 10mm, Left 15mm, Right 15mm
- **Content area:** 118mm × 190mm
- **Font size (body):** 9–10pt

### 80mm Thermal Receipt
- **Paper width:** 80mm
- **Print area width:** 72mm (leaving 4mm margin each side)
- **Font size (body):** 8pt (monospace preferred)
- **Font size (header/total):** 10pt bold
- **QR code size:** 40mm × 40mm centered
- **Character limit per line (12pt equiv):** ~32 characters
- **Max receipt length:** Variable (no page breaks — continuous roll)

### 58mm Thermal Receipt
- **Paper width:** 58mm
- **Print area width:** 48mm (leaving 5mm margin each side)
- **Font size (body):** 7–8pt
- **QR code size:** 28mm × 28mm centered
- **Character limit per line:** ~24 characters

## INVOICE SECTION SPECIFICATIONS

### Invoice Header Section
Required fields:
- Business name (largest font, bold)
- Business address (multi-line)
- Business phone and email
- Tax number (GST/VAT) if applicable
- Business logo (if uploaded)

### Invoice Info Section
Required fields:
- Invoice Number (prominent, right-aligned)
- Invoice Date
- Due Date (if credit sale)
- Invoice Type label

### Customer Section
Required fields (if customer provided):
- Customer name
- Customer address
- Customer tax number (if applicable)

### Line Items Table
Column widths for A4:
- # (5%) | Item Name (45%) | Qty (10%) | Rate (15%) | Tax% (10%) | Amount (15%)

Column widths for 80mm thermal:
- Item Name (50%) | Qty (15%) | Rate (35%)

### Invoice Totals Section
Required totals in order:
1. Subtotal
2. Discount (if applied)
3. Tax breakdown (CGST + SGST for India, or Tax label + amount)
4. Rounding (if applied)
5. **Grand Total** (largest, bold)
6. Amount Paid
7. Balance Due

### Invoice Footer
Required:
- "Thank you for your business"
- Payment terms (if credit)
- Disclaimer: "This is a system-generated document. Verify before use."
- "Powered by Aszurex | Sarang Business OS"

## FONT SPECIFICATIONS FOR PRINT

- **A4 / PDF exports:** Inter or Arial, with system font fallback
- **Thermal receipts:** Courier New (monospace) for column alignment
- **Minimum readable size:** 7pt for thermal, 9pt for A4
