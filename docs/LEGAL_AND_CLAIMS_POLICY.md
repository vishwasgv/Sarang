## TAX CALCULATION DISCLAIMER

Sarang includes a tax calculation engine that computes taxes based on:

- User-configured tax rates
- User-selected tax models (GST, VAT, Sales Tax, No Tax, Custom)
- Data entered by the user

Important:

Tax laws, rates, slabs, and regulations change frequently and vary by country, state, industry, and product category.

Sarang does NOT automatically update tax rates.

Sarang does NOT validate that the user's configured tax rates are legally correct.

Users are responsible for:

- Verifying current tax rates with a qualified tax professional
- Ensuring tax configurations are appropriate for their jurisdiction
- Reviewing all tax calculations before filing or remitting taxes
- Complying with local, state, and national tax laws

Sarang's tax engine is a calculation tool, not a tax compliance platform.

## PAYMENT RECORDING POLICY (PM005)

This rule is absolute and non-negotiable.

**SARANG RECORDS PAYMENTS. IT DOES NOT VERIFY OR PROCESS THEM.**

Permitted:

- Recording that a payment was received
- Recording the payment method (Cash, UPI, Card, Wallet, Credit)
- Recording the amount entered by the user
- Recording the date entered by the user
- Generating a UPI QR code containing merchant UPI ID and amount
- Displaying payment information on invoices and receipts

Not permitted:

- Claiming a payment was successful
- Confirming transaction completion
- Integrating with payment gateways
- Receiving, holding, or forwarding funds
- Acting as a financial intermediary
- Verifying UPI transactions automatically

Any future developer, AI agent, or contributor who attempts to add payment verification, payment gateway integration, or automatic transaction confirmation is in violation of this policy and must stop immediately.
