## INDIA DPDPA COMPLIANCE NOTE

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) governs personal data protection in India. Sarang's design is inherently aligned with DPDPA principles:

**No personal data is transmitted to Aszurex:** All business and customer data stays on the user's device. Aszurex does not receive, process, or store any personal data from Sarang installations.

**Data Fiduciary position:** The business owner (not Aszurex) is the Data Fiduciary for all customer personal data stored in Sarang. The business owner is responsible for:
- Collecting customer data lawfully and with appropriate consent
- Maintaining data accuracy and allowing correction
- Implementing appropriate physical and digital security on their device
- Honoring customer data access and deletion requests

**Aszurex's role under DPDPA:** Aszurex is not a Data Processor under DPDPA because it never receives data from Sarang. Aszurex provides software — the business owner processes data.

**Action before public distribution in India:** Consult a qualified legal professional to verify DPDPA compliance, especially if future versions introduce cloud features.
