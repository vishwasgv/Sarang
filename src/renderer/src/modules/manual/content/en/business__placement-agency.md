# Placement Agency

## What's included

Placement Agency is built on Sarang's shared service-business foundation — appointments, a service catalog, provider schedules, and the notification queue — plus a single dedicated module, **Placement Agency**, covering candidates, job orders from hiring companies, and the placements that connect them.

## Candidates

A candidate record carries full name, contact details, current job title and employer, total years of experience, skills and preferred locations (both freely tagged), an education summary and resume notes, current and expected salary, availability date, source (Walk-In, Referral, LinkedIn, Website, Other), and status (Active, Placed, On Hold, Blacklisted).

## Job Orders

A job order is an open requirement from a hiring company (one of your Sarang customers): job title and description, required skills, an experience range, a salary budget range, location, number of positions, a target date, and status (Open, In Progress, On Hold, Closed, Cancelled). Each job order also sets your **commission** — either a percentage of the candidate's annual salary, or a fixed amount — which Sarang uses to work out what you're owed once someone is placed against it.

## Placements

A placement links one candidate to one job order at one hiring company, with a joining date and offered salary. The commission amount is calculated automatically from the job order's commission rule (a percentage-type job order works out 12 months of the offered monthly salary × the percentage; a fixed-type job order just uses its fixed value), though you can override the amount before saving. A placement moves through **Offered → Joined → Invoiced** (with Cancelled as a separate outcome); **Generate Invoice** bills your commission (SAC 999132, 18% GST) once the candidate has joined, and a printable **Placement Confirmation Letter** is available for handing to the candidate.

The KPI bar shows active candidates, open job orders, placements this month, and revenue this month.

## Language

Placement Agency is one of Sarang's 24 dedicated service-business templates, and like nearly all of them its interface is **English only**, regardless of which language you've set elsewhere in Sarang.
