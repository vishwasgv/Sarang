# Company Secretary Practice

This business type's screens are in English only, regardless of your language setting elsewhere in Sarang.

## The shared service foundation

Every service-based business type in Sarang — including Company Secretary Practice — starts from the same four building blocks: **Appointments** (booking client meetings), a **Service Catalog** (the list of services and their prices), **Provider Schedules** (which CS/staff member is available when), and an automatic **Notification Queue** that handles reminders without you having to send them by hand. The rest of this chapter covers Sarang's tools for a CS practice: compliance deadline tracking, ROC filings, board meetings, and time tracking.

## Compliance Tasks

The **Compliance Tasks** screen works the same way described in the *CA Firm* chapter — a deadline tracker across every client with categories, priorities, and a Compliance Library of reusable templates, with KPI tiles for Overdue, Due Today, Due in 7 Days, and Filed/Done.

## ROC Filings & Meetings

This screen has two tabs specific to secretarial practice:

**ROC Filings** — track MCA filings per client: form type (MGT-7, AOC-4, DIR-12, INC-22, PAS-3, ADT-1, MBP-1, or Other), financial year, purpose, due date, status (Pending / In Progress / Filed / Acknowledged / Defective), government fee, and — once filed — the filing date and SRN (Service Request Number) from the MCA portal. Attach the filed form or acknowledgment receipt directly from the filing's edit form. KPI tiles show Pending Filings, Filed/Acknowledged, and Meetings in 30 Days.

**Board Meetings** — log board meetings, AGMs, EGMs, audit committee meetings, and NRC meetings per client, with date, time, venue, and agenda. Three checkboxes track **Notices Sent**, **Quorum Met**, and **Minutes Prepared**, each toggled with one click directly from the meeting list. Each meeting also has a **Resolutions** register — add numbered Ordinary or Special resolutions with their full text and whether they passed unanimously; leave the resolution number blank and Sarang auto-assigns the next number for that client. **Print Minutes** produces a formal minutes document with the meeting's agenda, quorum/notice/minutes status, and signature lines for the chairperson and company secretary. Attach supporting documents to a meeting from the same edit form.

### Compliance Rollup

The **Compliance Rollup** tab gives you an at-a-glance view of every company client's annual compliance status for a chosen financial year: whether the AGM was held (and when), and the filing status of MGT-7, AOC-4, and ADT-1 — each shown as a real status badge (Filed / Pending / Not Started) rather than something you have to piece together from separate screens.

## Time Entries

Time can be logged and billed the same way described in the *CA Firm* chapter's Time Entries section — from the standalone **Time Tracking** screen, filterable by staff, project, date range, and billed status, with **Generate Invoice** available on selected unbilled entries.
