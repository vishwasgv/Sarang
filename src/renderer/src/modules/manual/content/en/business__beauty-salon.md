# Beauty Salon

Beauty Salon is one of Sarang's 24 specific service-business templates. Like every business type in that group, this business type's screens are in English only, regardless of your language setting elsewhere in Sarang.

Every service-business template shares the same foundation: **Appointments** for booking, a **Service Catalog** of what you offer and at what price, **Provider Schedule** to define each staff member's working hours and generate real bookable time slots, and a background **Notification Queue** that sends appointment reminders. Beauty Salon adds three things on top of that base: session packs, staff commission, and multi-service booking.

## Multi-Service Booking

A salon appointment is rarely just one service — a client might get a haircut, a color, and a blow-dry in the same visit. Beauty Salon's booking form lets you add multiple services to a single appointment; Sarang adds up their prices and durations automatically and books one combined slot instead of forcing you to create separate appointments. Appointments can also carry a **chair assignment** so you know which station a client is booked into.

## Session Packs

A client can buy a bundle of prepaid sessions up front (e.g. "10-session hair spa package") instead of paying per visit. **Session Packs** lists every client's packs with how many sessions remain, flags packs that are running low (2 or fewer left) or already expired, and lets you search by client. When an appointment tied to a client with an active pack is marked **Completed**, Sarang automatically deducts one session from that pack instead of requiring a separate invoice — the appointment list marks these as "Paid via pack" rather than showing an invoice action.

## Staff Commission

When a completed appointment has a provider and an amount, Sarang can calculate that staff member's commission automatically (a default 10% of service revenue, though each staff member's actual rate is configurable in their Employee record). The **Commission** screen gives you a monthly report per staff member — revenue generated, commission earned, tips, and how much is paid vs. still pending — plus a full record-by-record list you can filter by staff or payment status and mark as paid in bulk once payouts are settled.
