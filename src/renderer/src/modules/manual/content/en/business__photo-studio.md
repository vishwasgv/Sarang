# Photo Studio

## What's included

Photo Studio is built on Sarang's shared service-business foundation — appointments, a service catalog, provider schedules, and the notification queue — plus a single dedicated module: **Shoot Bookings**. Unlike several other creative-vertical templates, Photo Studio does not add a separate Leads pipeline; a booking's own Inquiry status covers that instead.

## Shoot Bookings

Each booking records the client, a shoot type (Wedding, Maternity, Newborn, Birthday, Corporate, Product, Portfolio, Real Estate, or Other), the shoot date/time and location, estimated duration, expected photo count, a delivery deadline, one or more assigned photographers, and an assigned editor.

A booking moves through a status pipeline: **Inquiry → Confirmed → Shot → Editing → Delivered** (with Cancelled as a separate outcome) — a "Mark [next status]" button advances it one step at a time.

## Delivery tracking

Expanding a booking shows its **delivery milestones**, each toggled on/off with a timestamp: Proofs Sent to Client, Client Selection Received, Editing Started, Album Proof Sent, and Final Delivery Done. This gives you a simple checklist for where any given shoot's post-production stands.

## Billing

Once a final amount is entered on a booking, a **Generate Invoice** button turns it into a real invoice — Sarang won't let you generate a second invoice for the same booking. The KPI bar shows shoots this month, deliveries pending, and upcoming shoots.

## Language

Photo Studio is one of Sarang's 24 dedicated service-business templates, and like nearly all of them its interface is **English only**, regardless of which language you've set elsewhere in Sarang.
