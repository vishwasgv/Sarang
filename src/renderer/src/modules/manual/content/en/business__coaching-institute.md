# Coaching Institute

## What's included

Coaching Institute is built on Sarang's shared service-business foundation — appointments, a service catalog, provider schedules, and the notification queue — plus modules that together cover a full coaching workflow: **Students**, **Batches** (with a waitlist and a syllabus tracker), **Attendance**, **Fees**, **Test Scores**, **Performances & Recitals**, and a parent-facing **Progress Report**. This is one of the deepest business templates in Sarang, so it's worth walking through each piece.

## Students

**Students** is your roll book — each student profile carries a roll number, class/grade, school name, parent phone, enrollment date, and active/inactive status, layered on top of the same customer record used everywhere else in Sarang (so a student who's already a customer is found by phone search, not duplicated). Deactivating a student keeps their history; it doesn't delete anything.

Every student row has a **printer icon** — click it to generate a printable Progress Report for that student. It's built for handing to a parent: it lists every batch the student is enrolled in with their attendance percentage, all recorded test scores, and the fee status for each month, all on one page.

## Batches

**Batches** manages your class groups — batch name, subject/course, instructor, schedule (days of the week and time), room, maximum capacity, and monthly fee. Enrolling a student into a batch can apply a discount (type and amount), which produces that student's own effective monthly fee for that batch, distinct from the batch's list price.

**Waitlist.** Once a batch hits its maximum capacity, the "Enroll Student" button switches to "Join Waitlist" — the student is still added to the batch, just with Waitlisted status instead of Active, and isn't billed until promoted. The moment a seat opens up (a current student is dropped, or you raise the batch's capacity), a green promote icon appears next to each waitlisted student so you can move them to Active with one click.

**Syllabus.** Expand a batch and open its Syllabus panel to build a topic-by-topic checklist for that batch — add topics, check them off as you cover them, and a progress bar shows what fraction of the syllabus is actually done. Un-checking a topic clears its completion date, so the tracker always reflects what's really been taught, not just what was planned.

## Attendance

**Attendance** lets you pick a batch and a date, then mark each enrolled student present or absent with a single click (everyone defaults to present; toggle off the absent ones, or use Mark All Present/Absent). Re-opening a date you've already recorded loads the existing record so you can correct it. A printable attendance sheet is available for the selected batch and date.

## Fees

**Fees** generates a month's fee records for every active enrollment in one click, optionally applying a GST rate (a waitlisted enrollment is skipped automatically — it's only billed once promoted to Active). Each record shows amount due, amount received, and a status of Pending, Partial, Paid, or Waived, with overdue records flagged automatically once their due date has passed. You can mark a fee fully paid, edit a partial payment, or waive it outright, and print a fee receipt — which includes a UPI QR code for any remaining balance where UPI collection is set up.

## Test Scores

**Test Scores** (under Performance) records a test name, subject, marks obtained out of a max, test date, and grade for a specific student in a specific batch. Entering marks suggests a grade automatically on a common Indian A+–F scale, but the grade field stays free text — overwrite or clear it if your institute grades differently. The KPI bar shows tests recorded, the average score across all recorded tests, and how many results fall below 50%.

## Performances & Recitals

**Performances & Recitals** is a separate feature from Test Scores — it's for event and recital bookings, not academic marks. Use it for things like an annual day function, a music recital, or a public speaking showcase: record a performance name, date, venue, and which enrolled students from a batch are participating. It's a simple event roster, not a scored evaluation — pair it with Test Scores when a performance is also being graded.

## Language

Coaching Institute is one of Sarang's 24 dedicated service-business templates, and like nearly all of them its interface is **English only**, regardless of which language you've set elsewhere in Sarang.
