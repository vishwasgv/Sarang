# Gym / Fitness Studio

Gym / Fitness Studio is one of Sarang's 24 specific service-business templates. Like every business type in that group, this business type's screens are in English only, regardless of your language setting elsewhere in Sarang.

Every service-business template shares the same foundation: **Appointments** for booking, a **Service Catalog** of what you offer and at what price, **Provider Schedule** to define each trainer's working hours and generate real bookable time slots, and a background **Notification Queue** that sends appointment reminders. Gym/Studio adds four things on top of that base: session packs, memberships, batch classes, and staff commission.

## Memberships

**Memberships** is built around plans and check-ins, across several tabs:

- **All Memberships** — every member's membership with status (Active, Frozen, Expired, Cancelled), payment status (Paid, Pending, Partial), days remaining, and sessions used. **Freeze** an active membership with a reason (a real pause, not just a status flip) — its end date genuinely moves forward by however many days it stayed frozen once you **Resume** it, so a member never loses paid-for time. Also cancel a membership and generate its invoice directly from this list.
- **Expiring Soon** — every membership due to expire within a window you choose (7/14/30/60 days), so you can reach out for renewal before it lapses rather than after.
- **Plans** — the catalog of membership plans you sell: duration in days, price, an optional number of included sessions (leave blank for unlimited), and an optional list of classes the plan covers.
- **Quick Check-In** — a fast search-by-name-or-phone screen for the front desk to check an active member in without navigating anywhere else. Open **Attendance History** on any membership to see its full check-in log.

## Batch Classes (Group Classes)

For instructor-led group sessions — yoga, Zumba, spin, and similar — **Group Classes** lets you define a class with an instructor, a weekly schedule (pick the days and a time), a room/location, a capacity, and a start/end date. Each class shows a live capacity bar (enrolled vs. maximum) and turns red once full. From a class you can:

- **Manage enrollment** — add or remove members, blocked once the class hits capacity.
- **Mark attendance** — pick a session date and tick off which enrolled members showed up; attendance is saved per date and can be revisited later.

## Session Packs

The same prepaid-sessions mechanism used across Sarang's service verticals: a client buys a bundle of sessions up front, and **Session Packs** tracks how many remain per client, flagging packs running low or expired. Assign a **regular trainer** to a pack and Sarang pre-fills that trainer on the client's future bookings automatically — you can still pick someone else for a one-off substitute session, this is just a convenient default, never a lock-in.

## Staff Commission

When a completed, revenue-generating appointment has a trainer attached, Sarang can calculate that trainer's commission automatically (a default 10% of service revenue, with each staff member's actual rate configurable in their Employee record). The **Commission** screen gives a monthly report per staff member — revenue, commission, tips, paid vs. pending — plus a filterable record list you can mark as paid in bulk.
