# Getting Started

Sarang Business OS Lite is a free, offline business management app. Everything you record — invoices, customers, stock, staff — stays on this computer. Nothing is sent anywhere, and you never need an internet connection to use it.

## 1. Install

Run the installer and follow the prompts. No account, no sign-up, no internet connection required. Once installed, launching Sarang for the first time takes you straight to the Setup Wizard.

## 2. Setup Wizard

The Setup Wizard asks three things:

- **Business name** — shown on your invoices and printed documents.
- **Business type** — pick the option closest to what you actually do (Restaurant, Retail, Pharmacy, Hotel/Lodge, a professional-services type like Lawyer or Architect, and so on). This choice turns on the right features for you automatically — for example, a Restaurant gets Tables/KOT, a Pharmacy gets batch and expiry tracking, a Driving School gets learner profiles and session tracking. You can change this later in **Settings → Industry**, and you can also turn on extra cross-cutting features (like Returns, Barcode, or Logistics tracking) independently of your business type from **Settings → Additional Business Features**.
- **Currency** — Sarang supports about 150 world currencies with correct decimal handling for each.

## 3. Create your first customer and product

From the sidebar, open **Customers** and add one, or from **Products** add your first item. If a customer already exists (created from any other screen — Billing, an appointment booking, a hotel check-in, and so on), searching their phone number will find them again instead of creating a duplicate — this reuse-by-phone behavior works the same way everywhere in Sarang.

## 4. Create your first invoice

Open **Billing**, add products or services to the cart, pick (or quick-add) a customer, choose Cash or Credit, and confirm. The invoice appears immediately in your Invoices list with the correct total, tax, and any discount applied. Recording a payment against it (in full or in part) is done from the invoice's own detail screen.

That's the whole loop — most owners can go from a fresh install to their first recorded invoice in under fifteen minutes.

## 5. What's next

- **Reports** — Profit & Loss, Cash Book, Trial Balance, GST reports, and business-specific reports are all available from day one, exportable to PDF or Excel.
- **Backup** — set up a manual or scheduled backup from **Backup** so your data is protected. See the *Backup & Restore* chapter.
- **Users & Permissions** — if more than one person will use Sarang, add them as Users with a role that controls exactly what they can see and do. See the *Users & Permissions* chapter.
- **Ask Sarang** — once you're comfortable with the basics, turn on the AI Assistant from **Settings → Additional Business Features** to ask plain-English questions about your own business data. See the *Ask Sarang (AI Assistant)* chapter.

Every other chapter in this Manual is organized the same way: universal features that work the same for every business, followed by one chapter per business type covering what's specific to yours.
