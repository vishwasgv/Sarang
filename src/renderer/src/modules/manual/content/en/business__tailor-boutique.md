# Tailor / Boutique

## What's included

Tailor / Boutique is built on Sarang's shared service-business foundation — appointments, a service catalog, provider schedules, and the notification queue — plus a single dedicated module: **Tailoring Orders**, which covers both the order itself and each client's saved body measurements.

## Orders

Each order records the garment type (Shirt, Pant, Suit, Kurta, Salwar Kameez, Blouse, Lehenga, Saree Blouse, Jacket, Other), gender, style region (Indian or Western), who supplied the fabric (client or shop) and its description, quantity, unit price, and advance paid — with the balance due calculated automatically.

An order can be linked to one of the client's saved measurement records, and moves through a status pipeline: **Received → In Cutting → In Stitching → Trial Scheduled → (Alterations, if needed) → Ready → Delivered**, with Cancelled as a separate outcome. Trial and delivery dates are tracked separately, and overdue deliveries are flagged in red. Once Ready, a **Generate Invoice** button bills the order.

## Measurements

The **Measurements** tab keeps a running history of a client's body measurements — chest, waist, hips, shoulder, neck, sleeve, inseam, outseam, thigh, height, armhole, front/back neck depth, garment length, and cuff — along with who took the measurement and when. A client can have more than one measurement record over time, and any of them can be attached to a new order.

## Language

Tailor / Boutique is the **one deliberate exception** among Sarang's service-business templates: every other business type in this family runs in English only, but Tailor / Boutique's screens are fully translated and work in **all 13 of Sarang's supported languages**, the same as a product-based business like Retail or Pharmacy. Change your interface language from **Settings → Language** as normal — Tailoring Orders and Measurements will follow it.
