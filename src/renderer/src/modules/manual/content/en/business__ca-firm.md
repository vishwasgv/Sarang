# CA Firm

This business type's screens are in English only, regardless of your language setting elsewhere in Sarang.

## The shared service foundation

Every service-based business type in Sarang — including CA Firm — starts from the same four building blocks: **Appointments** (booking client meetings), a **Service Catalog** (the list of services and their prices), **Provider Schedules** (which CA/staff member is available when), and an automatic **Notification Queue** that handles reminders without you having to send them by hand. The rest of this chapter covers Sarang's tools for a CA practice: compliance deadline tracking, client engagements, and time tracking.

## Compliance Tasks

The **Compliance Tasks** screen is your firm's deadline tracker across every client — income tax, GST, TDS, ROC/MCA filings, audits, and anything else you define. KPI tiles show Overdue, Due Today, Due in 7 Days, and Filed/Done counts, so nothing slips through.

Add a task by picking a client, a title, category, due date, priority (Low/Normal/High/Urgent), and optionally assigning it to a staff member — or pick from your firm's **Compliance Library** of recurring event templates to auto-fill the title and category. Use **Update** on any task to move it through Pending → In Progress → Filed/Done, recording the filed-on date and an acknowledgment number once it's actually submitted, and attach the actual filed document or acknowledgment receipt. A note at the bottom of the screen reminds you that compliance dates shown here are for your own tracking convenience and should always be verified against the actual statutory calendar.

### AGM-relative filings and client document checklists

Open **Clients & Checklists** from the Compliance Tasks screen to set a client's **AGM date**. Once set, Sarang automatically generates MGT-7, AOC-4, and ADT-1 filing tasks with their correct statutory due dates (60/30/15 days after the AGM respectively) — you no longer need to calculate and enter these by hand. The AGM filing itself still needs to be added manually, since its own due date depends on board scheduling, not a fixed offset.

The same modal also holds a **document checklist** per client — track which documents (PAN, Aadhaar, Bank Statement, GST Certificate, or any custom item) have been collected. Use **Add Standard Checklist** to seed the 4 most common items in one click, then mark each Collected or Pending as documents come in.

## Engagements

**Engagements** track ongoing client relationships beyond one-off compliance tasks: retainers, audits, advisory work, and tax engagements. Each engagement has a title, type, fee structure (Fixed, Hourly, or Monthly Retainer with a chosen billing day of the month), start/end dates, and status (Active / Completed / Paused / Terminated). KPI tiles show Active Engagements, Monthly Retainer Revenue, and Fixed Fee Pipeline. Attach engagement letters and supporting documents directly from the edit form.

For any active engagement with a fee amount, **Generate Invoice** creates a real invoice for the current billing period — a monthly retainer can be re-invoiced every calendar month (it shows "Invoiced" for the current period once billed, and re-opens automatically the following month).

## Time Entries

The standalone **Time Tracking** screen logs billable hours against clients or projects — date, staff, description, hours, rate, computed amount — filterable by staff, project, date range, and billed status, with KPI tiles for Hours This Month, Unbilled Hours, and Unbilled Amount. Select unbilled entries and **Generate Invoice** to bill them directly; once billed, an entry can no longer be edited or deleted.
