# CA Firm

This business type's screens are in English only, regardless of your language setting elsewhere in Sarang.

## The shared service foundation

Every service-based business type in Sarang — including CA Firm — starts from the same four building blocks: **Appointments** (booking client meetings), a **Service Catalog** (the list of services and their prices), **Provider Schedules** (which CA/staff member is available when), and an automatic **Notification Queue** that handles reminders without you having to send them by hand. The rest of this chapter covers Sarang's tools for a CA practice: compliance deadline tracking, client engagements, and time tracking.

## Compliance Tasks

The **Compliance Tasks** screen is your firm's deadline tracker across every client — income tax, GST, TDS, ROC/MCA filings, audits, and anything else you define. KPI tiles show Overdue, Due Today, Due in 7 Days, and Filed/Done counts, so nothing slips through.

Add a task by picking a client, a title, category, due date, priority (Low/Normal/High/Urgent), and optionally assigning it to a staff member — or pick from your firm's **Compliance Library** of recurring event templates to auto-fill the title and category. Use **Update** on any task to move it through Pending → In Progress → Filed/Done, recording the filed-on date and an acknowledgment number once it's actually submitted. A note at the bottom of the screen reminds you that compliance dates shown here are for your own tracking convenience and should always be verified against the actual statutory calendar.

## Engagements

**Engagements** track ongoing client relationships beyond one-off compliance tasks: retainers, audits, advisory work, and tax engagements. Each engagement has a title, type, fee structure (Fixed, Hourly, or Monthly Retainer with a chosen billing day of the month), start/end dates, and status (Active / Completed / Paused / Terminated). KPI tiles show Active Engagements, Monthly Retainer Revenue, and Fixed Fee Pipeline.

For any active engagement with a fee amount, **Generate Invoice** creates a real invoice for the current billing period — a monthly retainer can be re-invoiced every calendar month (it shows "Invoiced" for the current period once billed, and re-opens automatically the following month).

## Time Entries

The standalone **Time Tracking** screen logs billable hours against clients or projects — date, staff, description, hours, rate, computed amount — filterable by staff, project, date range, and billed status, with KPI tiles for Hours This Month, Unbilled Hours, and Unbilled Amount. Select unbilled entries and **Generate Invoice** to bill them directly; once billed, an entry can no longer be edited or deleted.
